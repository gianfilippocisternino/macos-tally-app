Tally: Sales Bar for Mac

Your store's sales, always one click away.


Never miss a sale again

Tally lives in your Mac menu bar and shows your store's performance in real time. No browser tabs, no dashboards to load — just click the menu bar icon and see how your store is doing right now.


What you get

Revenue today
See today's total revenue with a comparison to yesterday at the same time.

Order count
Track how many orders have come in today and how you're trending.

Live revenue chart
An hourly breakdown of your sales throughout the day.

Live checkout tracking
See who's checking out right now — which products, where they're from, and how they found you.

Order notifications
Get a notification with sound every time a new order comes in. Never miss a sale, even when you're away from the browser.

Fulfilled orders
Keep track of how many orders have been shipped today.


Built for your Mac

Tally is a native macOS app — fast, lightweight, and private. It runs in your menu bar and uses minimal resources. Your data stays on your Mac and is never shared with third parties.
